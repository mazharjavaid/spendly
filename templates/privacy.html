Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly to track expenses, we store the data you enter: amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by category or date range.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to build profiles for any purpose outside the app.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data is stored on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and all associated expense records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces (DM Serif Display and DM Sans). This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when you visit the app. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle font requests.

We do not integrate with any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or other third-party data processors.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our site. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries as promptly as possible.
